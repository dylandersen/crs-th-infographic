The 8-Phase Claim Lifecycle

This flow details the Resident Specialist's journey from the initial adjuster call to the final handoff.

PHASE 1

Claim Creation

1 Locate Adjuster: Search Salesforce by name/phone to open Contact Page.
2 Initiate Claim: Click 'New' on the 'Claims' related list.
3 Enter Details: Fill in all known data (LOU, dates, PH info, specs).
4 Assign Teams: Use Chatter Maps to find and assign the correct RS/CCS.
5 Save Claim: System creates the claim and redirects to the Claim Page.
PHASE 2

Initial Insured Contact

1 Open Case: Receive notification and open the 'Housing Initial Call' case.
2 Attempt Contact: Call PH. If no answer, log 'Attempt Made' to auto-create follow-up tasks.
3 Conduct Call: Use 'Quick Text' template to guide conversational needs-gathering.
4 Confirm & Trigger: Update case status to 'Confirmed Housing', which auto-creates the 'Housing Placement' record.
5 Close Case: Mark the 'Housing Initial Call' case as 'Closed'.
PHASE 3

Post-Contact Setup

1 Consolidate Notes: Paste call notes into 'Additional Housing Call' case and set due date 1 year out to pin it.
2 Update Records: Update PH Contact (email, phone) and Claim (pet details, home specs).
3 Send PH Emails: Send 'What to Inspect' video and 'Housing Recap' templates.
4 Send THA: Send the Temporary Housing Agreement via the DocuSign task on the Claim Page.
KEY COMMUNICATION

24-Hour Adjuster Update

1 Prep: Get '24 Hour Update' template from S: Drive and review 'Adjuster Preferences' on Claim Page.
2 Send: Send email from Placement Page, editing template with PH needs and outstanding questions.
3 Log: Use 'Log a Call' to document any follow-up approvals (e.g., "Adjuster approved 3-month term").
PHASE 4

Housing Search

1 Tool 1 (Atom): Use Atom tool to find MLS options and 'Create Search Pad' automatically.
2 Tool 2 (Manual): Use Zillow/other sites and manually 'New Search Pad' record.
3 Vet: Call landlord to confirm availability, rent, and terms. Check distance on Google.
4 Log Confirmation: Update the 'Landlord Confirmation' task and set Search Pad status to 'Available' or 'Unavailable'.
PHASE 5

Property Vetting (PSL)

1 Move to PSL: Change Search Pad stage to 'PSL' (Property Short List).
2 Complete Details: Fill out the 'PSL Details' tab (dates, appliances, utilities, pet status).
3 Add Costs: Use 'Cost Editor' to add all fees, rent, and note deposit responsibilities in comments.
4 Pre-Present (If Needed): Send 'Actionable Request' or manual email (with S: Drive pricing) to adjuster for approval.
5 Log Approval: 'Log a Call' on the Search Pad with the adjuster's email approval.
PHASE 6

Presentation & Viewing

1 Set Viewing: Call PH to present the approved property and schedule a viewing time.
2 Update Task: Set the 'Viewing Due Date' on the 'Results of Viewing' task.
3 Get Feedback: Call PH after the viewing to get their decision.
4 Handle Decision: If Declined, log reason and return to Phase 4. If Accepted, check 'Accept Property'.
5 Trigger Acceptance: Checking 'Accept Property' creates the 'PH Accepted' task.
PHASE 7

Application & Acceptance

1 Track Application: Follow up with landlord and 'Log a Call' when applications are approved.
2 Create Furniture Order: On Placement Page, 'Add Furniture Order', fill in all details, and 'Save' (Status: Working).
3 Send COT: Use 'Quick Text' on Search Pad to send 'Confirmation of Terms' to landlord (CC the MIS).
4 Set Expectations: Call PH to explain MIS handoff, hotel checkout, and utility setup.
PHASE 8

Handoff to Move-In Specialist

1 Finalize Search Pad: Double-check all dates (move-in, furniture) and details for accuracy.
2 Complete 'PH Accepted' Task: Fill in the embedded template with all final details (THA status, RSD plan, payable info).
3 Reassign Task: Change 'Assigned To' on the 'PH Accepted' task to the designated MIS.
4 Reassign Claim: Change the 'Claim Owner' on the main Claim Page to the MIS.
